About

A Week-long Hackathon from 1st to 7th December 2021,
where the participants are expected to develop a viable prototype
based on the given problem statement in any one
of the tracks. Three tracks will be provided Participants
can only take part as a team of 2-4 members.




Timeline

1

Registration Starts

2

Registration Ends

3

Ideate Phase

Participants are required to submit their ideas as a PDF/PPT/Notion Doc on or before the first day briefing their problem statement, solution and the stack used to implement the same.
Participants are supposed to mention their team details in the document to be submitted.

4

Development Phase

The participants will be asked to develop a software prototype (Website/App).
The event will follow comprehensive evaluation and hence, timely report submissions at the given deadlines in the prescribed format will also add to the final scores of the competition. Teams will be shortlisted on the basis of their performance

5

Presentation Phase

Teams should give a presentation on their project along with a demonstration of their working prototype in front of the judges on the presentation day which will be the final judgment of the event.
